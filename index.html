Information about Systems, Plurality, Alters, etc

Plurality is when there are multiple identities within one brain.

The experience of being plural is varied in many ways, with different systems experiencing it differently.

This source aims to give a starting point for anyone unfamiliar with systems, to go over how to interact with and be respectful to systems and understand the basics.
